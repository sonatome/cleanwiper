The Ultimate Termite Guide

Published: September 15, 2024

Termites are silent destroyers, often causing extensive damage before they are even noticed. Understanding the early signs and knowing how to prevent them is crucial for protecting your most valuable assetâ€”your home.

Recognizing the Early Signs

  • **Mud Tubes:** Subterranean termites build narrow tunnels made of mud and saliva, often found near the foundation.
  • **Swarmers:** Finding discarded wings (often near windows or doors) is a clear indication that reproductive termites have swarmed.
  • **Damaged Wood:** Wood that sounds hollow when tapped, or paint that bubbles or cracks may signify termite tunneling underneath.
  • **Frass (Termite Droppings):** Drywood termites leave behind small, pellet-like droppings, usually near infested wood.

Professional Treatment Options

If you suspect an infestation, professional inspection and treatment are essential. CleanSweep Pest Control offers:

  1. **Liquid Barrier Treatments:** Applying a termiticide barrier around the perimeter of the home to prevent entry.
  2. **Baiting Systems:** Strategically placing termite bait stations in the ground to eliminate the entire colony over time.
  3. **Wood Treatment:** Direct application of specialized chemicals to infested or vulnerable areas of wood.

Don't wait until the damage is visible. Regular inspections can save you thousands of dollars in repairs.
